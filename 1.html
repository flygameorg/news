Multiple Minecraft Players Report Worlds Being Replaced by Unknown Save File

Published June 29, 2026 • By Staff Reporter

An increasing number of Minecraft players have reported an unusual issue in which existing single-player worlds are allegedly replaced by an unfamiliar save file.

According to community posts, the replacement world automatically enables LAN multiplayer and prevents players from changing game settings. Several users claim the world reappears even after deletion.

"No official explanation has been provided, and the reports remain unverified."

Discussion surrounding the phenomenon intensified after community members noticed that several players who originally documented the issue stopped posting online only days later.

Meanwhile, affected users claim the mysterious world continues expanding over time, with identical structures appearing across multiple independent copies of the save.
